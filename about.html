About Me

Fell in love with gaming due to game consoles my parents left around. I started with RPGMaker and Gamemaker, learning how to make smaller scale games when I was nine years old. In high school my teacher started a game design class so I started to learn Unity before the next school year to better prepare myself.


Right now I make various video games, be it a game jam or a personal project. Besides that I'm an advisor for Developers of Dearborn, coaching the club through project management and challenges game development has. I'm currently studying cybersecurity and computer science.


My ambition is to become a project manager, producer or director full time in the video game industry. It will enable me to pursue my vision of games to light

About

"What does it take for a peaceful date? Our Pink Hero, Scarlet, just wants to have a happy date with her favorite engineer, Patty but with Municipal drones going haywire and a jealous teammate on the rise... Scarlet just can't catch a break! Platform your way through the cityscape to stop the enemies and get back in time for dessert!"

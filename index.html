Phantom Rhythm Girl

Phantom Rhythm Girl is a rhythm fencing game made for the Touhou Pride Game Jam 3 over the course of 15 days.

After an argument about Youmu's training habits, Reisen has challenged her girlfriend to a duel. Help Youmu train and overcome her differences with Reisen through rhythmic fencing action!
